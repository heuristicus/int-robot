What Was The Assignment?

At a high level the aim of this assignment was to build a system for the robot to use in order to find an empty meeting room in which the meeting can occur and then proceed to invite and guide people to the meeting. At a lower level, this assignment can be further broken down into sub goals. The first part of the task is to explore and find an empty meeting room. So the robot was required to explore the open space and then ensure it is empty. Once an empty room had been found and identified, the next task for the robot was to begin exploring the map to find people to invite and guide to the meeting room it has identified as empty. To identify people, face detection can be used or laser data can be used to identify legs or any other means which successfully identify people. Once the robot has guided a person to the meeting room, it is to continue exploring for more people indefinately, or until the number of people who were to be found are actually found and in the meeting room.


Problems To Consider For The Assignment?

Bullet pointed below are some of the problems which need to be considered for this task:

  • How to explore to find people and meeting rooms ?
  • How to validate if meeting room is empty ?
  • How to detect people ?
  • How get to a person who has been detected ?
  • How to take people who have accepted the invite back to the meeting room?
  • How to ensure robot knows its location at all times
